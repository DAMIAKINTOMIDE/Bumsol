About us

school

Bumsol is an educational institution that provides the foundational knowledge for children.We have various facilities to provide a conducive environment for our pupils. Activities that not only educate the children, but help them relax. We at bumsol, put our pupils first in everything and ensure to give each student attention including space to grow as an individual and together.

Founder & Principal

Mrs Bunmi Ayandokun. Founder and Pincipal at Bumsol started this school in 2013 to provide children at the Ibadan axis an educational background and meeans to fulfil their true potential.

Vision

remove_red_eye

At bumsol we look to improve the quality of school with entrepreneurial skills and provide every means for our pupils to grow in their own way which is best suitable for them.

Mission

Our mission is to develop the young with active and creative minds, a sense of understanding and compassion for others, and the courage to act on their beliefs. We stress the total development of each child: moral, intellectual, social, emotional, and physical.
